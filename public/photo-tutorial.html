Taking the Perfect Photo

Getting a high-quality, clear photo is the most important step for an accurate Aura score. Follow these tips to get the best results:

  • Lighting is Key: Use soft, even lighting. Avoid harsh shadows or bright, direct light. Natural daylight from a window is often best.
  • Look Directly at the Camera: Your face should be pointed straight at the camera. Avoid tilting your head up, down, or to the side.
  • Neutral Expression: Try to maintain a neutral facial expression. Avoid smiling, frowning, or making other expressive faces.
  • Hair Off Your Face: Make sure your hair is pulled back and not covering any part of your face, especially your forehead, eyes, and jawline.
  • Clear Background: Use a simple, uncluttered background. This helps the AI focus on your face without distractions.
