Understanding Your Score

Your Aura score is a percentage that represents the symmetry of your face. Here's what the score means:

  • 90-100%: Nearly perfect symmetry. This is very rare and is considered the ideal of facial beauty.
  • 80-89%: Very high symmetry. This is considered very attractive.
  • 70-79%: High symmetry. This is considered attractive.
  • 60-69%: Above average symmetry.
  • 50-59%: Average symmetry.
  • Below 50%: Below average symmetry.
