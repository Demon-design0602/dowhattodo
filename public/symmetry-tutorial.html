Improving Your Symmetry

While genetics play a large role in facial symmetry, there are some things you can do to improve it over time:

  • Sleep on Your Back: Sleeping on your side or stomach can cause your face to become asymmetrical over time.
  • Chew on Both Sides: Chewing on only one side of your mouth can lead to an imbalance in your facial muscles.
  • Good Posture: Poor posture can cause your head to tilt, which can lead to an asymmetrical appearance.
  • Facial Exercises: Certain facial exercises can help to strengthen and tone your facial muscles, which can improve symmetry.
